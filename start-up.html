    Why it matters

    The paperwork you skip today becomes the penalty letter tomorrow.

    Choosing a name, registering with HMRC or Companies House, appointing officers, and knowing when PAYE kicks in are not side quests — they are the foundation every bank, client and inspector will check.

    Dinson & Co. exists so founders in Nottingham and across the UK can start trading with the right structure, the right registrations, and a named accountant who already knows the plan.

    What we cover

    Four pillars of a clean launch

    Each service stands alone or stacks together — most founders need at least two from day one.

    01

    Company Formations

    We guide name choice, SIC codes, share structure, directors and registered office — then file your incorporation with Companies House so your limited company exists on day one of the plan, not week six of guessing.

    • Name availability & structure advice
    • Electronic Companies House filing
    • Share certificates & first registers
    02

    Company Secretarial Services

    Once incorporated, statutory duties never pause. We keep registers current, file confirmation statements, and update Companies House when people or addresses change — so compliance stays invisible until you need a board pack.

    • Confirmation statements
    • Director & PSC updates
    • Statutory books maintenance
    03

    Self-Employed Registration

    Going freelance or trading in your own name? We register you with HMRC as a sole trader, explain National Insurance Class 2/4, and set up a financial structure that scales if you later incorporate.

    • HMRC self-employment setup
    • Tax & NI orientation
    • Path to limited company later
    04

    PAYE Registration

    Hiring staff or taking a director’s salary means an employer PAYE scheme. We register you with HMRC, connect payroll, and make sure the first payday is RTI-ready — then hand over to ongoing payroll services if you want.

    • Employer PAYE scheme setup
    • First payroll readiness
    • HMRC employer reference handled
