CIS

As a Chartered Certified accounting firm, we specialize in providing comprehensive CIS (Construction Industry Scheme) services tailored to the unique needs of contractors and subcontractors within the construction industry. Our firm understands the intricacies of CIS regulations and the impact they have on your business operations. With our expert guidance and support, you can rest assured that your CIS compliance requirements are handled with precision and efficiency.

Our CIS services encompass all aspects of the scheme, from initial registration and verification to ongoing compliance support. We assist contractors and subcontractors with the registration process, ensuring that all necessary verification checks are carried out to confirm the status of subcontractors. Our team provides personalized support and advice to help you maintain accurate records, file CIS returns, and meet reporting requirements seamlessly.

Additionally, we offer specialized tax planning and advice tailored to the construction industry, helping you optimize your tax position while adhering to CIS regulations. Our goal is to help you navigate the complexities of CIS with ease, identify and mitigate risks, and provide representation in the event of HMRC inquiries or disputes related to the scheme.

At Dinson & Co, we are committed to delivering high-quality CIS services that empower our clients to achieve compliance and operational excellence within the construction industry.
