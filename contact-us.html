Frequently asked

Answers before you ask

How quickly does Dinson & Co. respond to enquiries?

We aim to respond to every call and message within one business day. For anything urgent, calling the office directly is the fastest way to reach a member of the team.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes. New clients are welcome to a free, no-obligation introductory call to talk through their accounts, tax position, or business plans before deciding whether to go ahead.

What areas do you serve?

Our office is based in Carlton, Nottingham, and we work with clients across Nottinghamshire in person, as well as clients across the UK remotely using secure online tools.

Can I switch accountants partway through the tax year?

Yes — you can switch at any point in the year. We handle the handover directly with your previous accountant, including requesting professional clearance and prior records, so the move is straightforward for you.

Is Dinson & Co. regulated?

Yes. Dinson & Co. operates as ACCA Chartered Certified Accountants, bound by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants' professional and ethical standards.
