Who We Are

About Dinson & Co.

At Dinson & Co., we believe great accounting is about more than balancing the books—it's about helping businesses make smarter financial decisions and achieve sustainable growth. As a Nottingham-based Chartered Certified Accountancy practice with over 20 years of experience, we provide reliable, proactive, and personalised financial solutions tailored to the needs of businesses and individuals across the UK.

From accounting and bookkeeping to tax planning, payroll, Making Tax Digital (MTD) compliance, and business advisory, our experienced team delivers practical advice that keeps you compliant, improves efficiency, and helps you maximise every financial opportunity.

Whether you're launching a new business, expanding an established company, or looking for expert guidance to optimise your finances, we're committed to being your trusted long-term partner—providing clarity, confidence, and strategic advice that supports your success at every stage.
